About this app

The ultimate Material 3 calculator suite. Calc OS packs everything you need: a powerful Scientific & Programmer calculator, 15+ real-time converters (Currency, Crypto, Health, Financial), and an intelligent AI Math Assistant that formats answers in perfect LaTeX. Fully customizable with Material You dynamic theming.

Pro Modes

Scientific trig, logs, bitwise ops, and HEX/DEC/OCT/BIN swapping.

15+ Converters

Live currency, length, data, speed, BMI, and loan amortization.

Material You

Deep personalization. Custom fonts, button shapes, and dynamic colors.

How We Work

Data-Label Decoupling ke asool par chalta engine

01

Static Label Generation

Sab se pehle, shopkeeper har product ke liye aik unique barcode generate karta hai. Unique ID: Yeh barcode sirf aik Product ID (SKU) store karta hai, qeemat nahi. One-Time Print: Aap ko sticker sirf aik dafa print kar ke lagana parta hai.

02

The Cloud Brain

Jab shopkeeper system mein kisi item ki detail daltay hain, toh qeemat database mein store ho jati hai. Dynamic Control: Market mein qeemat barhti hai, toh sirf dashboard se qeemat tabdeel karni parti hai. Real-time Update: System foran nayi qeemat dikhane lagta hai.

03

High-Speed Interaction

Jab customer counter par ata hai: Instant Scan: Barcode reader sticker ko scan karta hai. Assembly-Level Fetching: Raqvo engine foran database se latest price uthata hai. Bidirectional Action: Bill generate hota hai aur stock update ho jata hai.

04

Smart Monitoring

Background tasks: Expiry Watchman: Stock ki dates check karta hai aur expiry alerts bhejta hai. Business Intelligence: Har sale ke sath, real-time mein total revenue aur munafa (profit) dikhata rehta hai.

What Makes Us Different

Raqvo stands out from traditional POS systems by replacing rigid hardware-dependent workflows with a high-performance, flexible digital engine. Standard systems require re-printing every item when prices change. Raqvo introduces Data-Label Decoupling.

Built by HyBox, it prioritizes execution over complex software. Engineered in Vanilla JS to ensure an "Assembly-level" feel, providing direct instantaneous hardware interaction without the lag found in heavy frameworks.
